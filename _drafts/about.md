##ABOUT


Plato Frugal - rethinking food.
This is a personal food blog focused on holistic lifestyle compatible food recipes with potentially minimal effort in time, space and money.
Its not devoted to any particular diet - presumably the trend of "clean eating" would fit best the description.

- As...
- natural
- clean (e.g. favour raw milk over pasteurized milk)
- easy
- suitable for everyday use
- time-saving
- elementary
- simply frugal
- ...as possible.

To be fair to the concept of frugality, I'll save the long stories within the posts and focus on the main content.

DISCLAIMER The recipes do not provide any kind of nutritional advice. Since i am no professional dietary adviser/ nutritionist i will not give any individual advice.
This blog represents exclusively what works for me personally. Take it or leave it ;) 



###RECIPES

Plato Frugal is not devoted to any particular diet since diets are man-made concepts often based on any kind of belief.  
Therefore every recipe has ingredients-based tags (e.g. gluten-free, refined sugar-free).
...
TAGS: by function, not belief: dairy-free, gluten-free, refined sugar-free, snack, meal, fishy friday, 
meatless monday

TAGS beziehen sich auf Main Angaben.


###WORKOUTS

Legend:

AMRAP: As many repitions as possible
DNOR: Decreasing number of repitions (per round)
INOR: Increasing number of repitions (per round)

TRAIN LIKE... LEON DER PROFI, etc.


In addition to the recipes as a kind of a dualistic counterpart ("burn the fuel") i will release workout plans of my own training sessions. 
Maybe theyll serve your inspiration.  
These workouts are primarily based on the concept of HIIT respectively HIT and require either no or minimal equipment
so that most of the workouts are feasible at any place to meet the idea of frugality.
